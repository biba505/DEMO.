Welcome to Classic Motor Cars


From our inception in 1993 we have built an international reputation for classic car restoration, preservation and conservation. Our team have restored and maintained some of Jaguar's most important road and race cars over the years. We have been fortunate enough to receive the Historic Motoring Awards 'Restoration Of The Year' twice, in 2011 and 2017. A feat that no other company has done globally. In 2021, we were honoured to receive the 'Specialist Of The Year' Award to sit alongside our RAC Historic Awards 'Restoration Of The Year' for the Aston Martin Bulldog.

Our "all under one roof philosophy" gives us total quality control. Virtually all the work we carry out is done here on site at our headquarters in Bridgnorth Shropshire. From paint and panel, to trim, engine builds and all other mechanical and electrical work.
